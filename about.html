About Me

A few fun facts

Hi! My name is Melissa Guerra and I am currently in my junior year at the University of Florida in Gainesville, Fla. I am working towards a degree in Advertising with an outside concentration in Communications Studies. I am an active member of Delta Zeta Sorority and have held positions such as Public Relations Chair. I really enjoyed this position because I was responsible for all of Delta Zeta's social media platforms and for reporting our accomplishments to our national publication, The Lamp.

I have also been involved with Dance Marathon at University of Florida, an organization that works year round to raise funds for Children's Miracle Network. Freshman year I was chosen as a dancer for Delta Zeta's team and stood for 26.2 hours all FTK (For The Kids). After expereiencing the event, I knew that I wanted to get involved with DM in any way I could. So sophomore year I interviewed and was selected as a Community Events Captain. The Community Events team is responsible for planning all the events leading up to Dance Marathon in the spring. I am now in my second year as a captain and could not be happier to be involved in such a wonderful organization. All funds raised are donated to UFHealth Shands Hospital right here in Gainesville. You can find out more information about Dance Marathon at UF right here and if you would like to donate, feel free to visit my personal donation page.

I also work part-time at Pinner's Fine Shoes as a sales associate and social media manager. Pinner's is certainly a staple of the Gainesville community and is currently in its 46th year of retail. Working at Pinner's has been such a great experience and one that has made my college career very unique. I have had the opportunity to meet so many great people and learn a lot.

In my spare time, I love enjoying time with friends, spending days at the beach and running. I completed the Marine Corps Marathon alongside my dad in 2011 and plan to train for more races in the near future. To get back in the swing of things, I will be running the Gasparilla Half-Marathon in February in my hometown of Tampa, Florida. I also love traveling and can't wait for my next adventure in Italy this summer! There are many places I have yet to go and can't wait to check them off my list.

I appreciate you visiting my website. To get in touch with me, please visit the contact page!
